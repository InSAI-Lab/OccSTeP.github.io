Overview

OccSTeP focuses on spatio-temporal persistence for 4D occupancy prediction: models should maintain stable scene understanding despite temporal disruptions and noisy observations. We provide a benchmark suite and a world-model baseline designed for incremental, long-horizon modeling.

Key Contributions

  • OccSTeP Benchmark: evaluates temporal persistence under missing frames, view fragmentation, backward playback, and semantic noise.
  • OccSTeP-WM: a tokenizer-free world model with dense voxel state representation and linear-complexity sequence modeling.
  • Online / Incremental 4D Modeling: supports reactive prediction and action-conditioned forecasting via recurrent state-space fusion.
